Job Purpose

As a Research Engineer at OpenAI, you will play a crucial role in building AI systems that can accomplish tasks that were previously considered impossible or achieve unprecedented levels of performance. We are seeking individuals who possess strong engineering skills, including designing, implementing, and enhancing large-scale distributed machine learning systems. Additionally, you will be responsible for writing bug-free machine learning code and contributing to the development of the scientific foundation behind the algorithms utilized.

Job Duties

  • Collaborate with researchers to develop and implement machine learning models and algorithms
  • Design and construct scalable and efficient distributed machine learning systems
  • Write clean, maintainable, and bug-free machine learning code
  • Conduct thorough testing and debugging to ensure the reliability and accuracy of AI systems
  • Iterate and improve existing machine learning algorithms for enhanced performance
  • Contribute to the research community through publications and presentations
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in machine learning and related fields

Required Qualifications

Education

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field. A Master's or Ph.D. degree is preferred.

Experience

  • Proven experience in designing, implementing, and improving large-scale distributed machine learning systems
  • Strong programming skills in languages such as Python, C++, or Java
  • Familiarity with deep learning frameworks, such as TensorFlow or PyTorch
  • Solid understanding of machine learning algorithms and techniques

Knowledge and Skills

  • Strong problem-solving and analytical abilities
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work effectively in a collaborative research environment
  • Solid understanding of software engineering principles and best practices
  • Proficiency in working with large datasets and distributed computing frameworks

Preferred Qualifications

  • Advanced degree (Master's or Ph.D.) in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field
  • Research experience in the field of artificial intelligence and machine learning
  • Published research papers or contributions to open-source machine learning projects
  • Familiarity with cloud computing platforms, such as AWS or Google Cloud Platform

Working Conditions

  • This position is full-time and based in our San Francisco, California office.
  • Collaboration with researchers and engineers in a fast-paced and dynamic environment.
  • Occasional travel may be required for conferences, workshops, or research collaborations.
